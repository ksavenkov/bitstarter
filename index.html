The economy itself is aimed to reach equilibria using money as a communication tool. The money are used to communicate and align efforts of many people towards the common goal. While creating and facilitating this process, the money itself don't solve the main problem of building the better future. Before the effort may be applied and supported, the intents of many people must align. One who has a problem should find and reach one who may come up with the solution, they need to engage a lot of value chains to make the cogs of modern economy moving, producing the solution. This process creates an additional value, which is used to solve other problems.

Imagine that all this friction may go away thanks to a new tool of communication, which enables people seamlessly express and align their efforts to solve common problems, creating a cascade of value chain shortcuts and liberating the society both from the friction of money-driven economy and state regulations.

Imagine the collective mind that fully understands their capabilities and needs. Which control and lead the conventional economy in a similar way as your neural system drives your body. If any need may be solved within this organism, the request gets a feedback with a speed of an impulse going through nerve, and is fulfilled shortly afterwards. What can't be fulfilled by the body itself, gets it priority and solved ASAP by combined effort of many sells under the unite, though distributed control. Would you like to become a cell in such an organism?

We provide you a convenient tool to express your needs, find peers who may help you, control your identity and anonimity. Align your efforts with other people across the globe. Spend your attention fully on pursuing the better future and not to oil the rusty cogs of old, high-friction, money-powered economy.
